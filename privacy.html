Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

The Smart Layer ("we," "us," or "our") operates https://thesmartlayer.com. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and their customers in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

2. Information We Collect

We collect contact details (name, email, phone number), business data for AI Voice Agent training, and technical data (IP addresses, usage patterns) when you interact with our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to perform AI-driven automation (scheduling, lead sync, invoicing), optimize your digital visibility, and communicate service updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze website traffic, optimize our platform, and run targeted advertising campaigns. You can control your cookie preferences through your internet browser settings.

5. AI and Third-Party Processing

To provide our "Digital Coworker" services, your data may be processed securely by third-party infrastructure providers including Anthropic (Claude API), Vapi, Airtable, and Netlify. These providers are used solely for the technical execution of your automation workflows.

6. International Data Transfers

Because our third-party AI infrastructure is primarily based in the United States, your data may be transferred, stored, and processed outside of Canada. By using our services, you consent to these necessary cross-border data transfers.

7. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. Information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or as required by Canadian law.

8. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a security breach that poses a real risk of significant harm to your personal information, we will promptly notify you and the Privacy Commissioner of Canada in accordance with PIPEDA requirements.

9. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
